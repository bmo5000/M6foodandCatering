About Me, The Food Man


My name is Marshawn Howard and I was raised in the Bay Area. I came from a broken home my mother was an addict and my father didnt claim me, but thanks to my auntie and late grandmother, im still alive today. I watch those women make mostly everything from scratch. My passion for cooking came later on in life when I was sentence to a 7 year federal bid. There I learned how to make homemade pizza dough, ice cream, icy, cheese cakes, and other fried foods, all with a microwave. I've done a 10 week culinary training at Saint Vincent DE Paul, and now I'm currently enrolled at Laney College in Oakland, CA. My goal is to raise money for a food truck.
